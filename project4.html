Color Coded, A Mobile Puzzle Game

October 2016 - Current

The code for and a sample of this project can be found here.

Color Coded is a Puzzle Game designed around placing hexes adjacent to one another, to clear like colors and combine colors according to the color wheel. This game has 100 levels as of now, and it is stil being developed, and will eventually be available for download on Mobile devices. Our development team is currently in the running for The Microsoft Imagine Cup 2016 Competition with this project.

My Role

During the process of making this game, I created the overall Interface of the program, including the menu systems, button layout, color scheme, and all animations. I had also programmed the mechanic that allows the player to pick up Hexes and place them into the puzzle, one of the main mechanic of our game.

This was a group project and could not have been accomplished without my group members, John Palermo, Robert Bailey, Noah Kellem, and Lucas Hedrick.
