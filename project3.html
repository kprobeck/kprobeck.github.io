inDICEsion

August 2016 - Current

inDICEsion [in-die-cision] is a dexterity game about throwing and rolling foam dice. Players must roll onto their boards in an attempt to score points equal to their roll, but can also throw their dice at opponent boards to knock down their opponent’s score. (A Rules Sheet for Adapterra can be found here.)

My Role

During the process of making this board game, I worked on balancing the mechanics and abilities in the game, as to not create a dominant or game breaking strategy. I also helped create artwork for the game, including the Boxart and Design Document, which can be found here.

This was a group project and could not have been accomplished without my group members, Robert Bailey, Noak Kellem, John Palermo, and Luke Hedrick.
