Unity Park Ride - Alien Ship

October 2015

In this project, I had created a simple pendulum-type ride in a Unity scene that used C# code to move back and forth at a steady pace. I had created the ride entirely out of Unity primitives, and created a terrain with specific lighting to create the scene I was looking for in this demonstration of an eerie setting.
